Press Foundation launches ‘AI Era News Copyright Forum’.

The AI ​​Era News Copyright Forum was launched to create a healthy media environment by preparing response plans to changes in the digital environment, such as news copyright protection and generative artificial intelligence (AI) technology. It will be hosted by the Korea Press Foundation and jointly organized by the Korea Newspaper Association, Korea Online Newspaper Association, Korea Broadcasting Association, Korea Journalists Association, Korea Newspaper Broadcasting Editors Association, and Korea Internet Newspaper Association.

Seoul Metropolitan Government, training 6,000 artificial intelligence (AI) startups with Google.

The first class of ‘Artificial Intelligence (AI) Startup School with Seoul’ hosted by the Seoul Metropolitan Government and Google will begin on March 5 (Tuesday). This is an education jointly conducted by the Seoul Metropolitan Government and Google to foster early and prospective entrepreneurs in the field of artificial intelligence (AI), a key industry of the future. This is the first case of a city in the world conducting education in cooperation with Google.
