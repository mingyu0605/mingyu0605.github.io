League of Legends

This game is A game in the MOBA genre where five players each grow in different positions to raise items and levels to destroy the opponent's base. It is also called an abbreviation such as LoL, which takes the first letters of the game name, and League in English-speaking countries.

It gained great popularity by lowering the barrier to entry compared to previous MOBA (AOS) games and currently has a large number of users around the world. It is one of the most played PC games in the world, and as of 2016, the number of monthly players reached more than 100 million, and as of August 2019, the combined number of concurrent users at peak hours on servers around the world per day was more than 8 million.

In addition, numerous e-sports competitions are being held, including the League of Legends World Championship, which holds the record for the largest number of viewers among e-sports competitions around the world, and regional leagues. It was also adopted as an official demonstration sport at the 2018 Jakarta-Palembang Asian Games.

Domestic In Korea, it is the most popular PC game that has always ranked first in the PC room market share. It is consistently loved mainly by teenagers and young adults, so it goes beyond simply being a PC game and has become one of the symbolic elements of Korean male youth culture along with European soccer.
