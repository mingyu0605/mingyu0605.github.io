league of legends

This game is A game in the MOBA genre where five players grow in different positions to increase items and levels to destroy the opponent's base.

It is also abbreviated as LoL after the preceding spelling of the game's name, and League in the English-speaking world.

It has gained high popularity by lowering the barrier to entry compared to previous MOBA (AOS) games, and now it has a huge number of users all over the world. It is one of the most played PC games in the world, with more than 100 million monthly players in 2016 and more than 8 million concurrent daily concurrent users on its servers worldwide as of August 2019. In addition, numerous esports competitions are being held, such as the League of Legends World Championship, which holds the record for the highest number of viewers among esports competitions in the world, and regional leagues. It was also adopted as an official demonstration sport at the 2018 Asian Games in Jakarta and Palembang.

It is the most popular PC game that has always ranked first in the share of PC bangs in Korea, and it has been steadily loved mainly by teenagers and young adults, so it has established itself as one of the symbols of Korean youth culture along with European soccer beyond just PC games.

The beginning of Chinese games and Riot Games under Tencent in China started in California, USA, and later Riot Games was 100% sold to Tencent in China, and now Riot Games is under Tencent, a Chinese IT company.
